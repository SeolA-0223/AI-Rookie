# Municipal Case Pack

These case packs are normalized demo datasets grounded in official municipality ordinance pages on the Ministry of Government Legislation site.

- The ordinance titles, municipalities, and promulgation/effective dates come from the official source pages listed in each `meta.json`.
- The clause texts are normalized demo excerpts for pipeline evaluation. They are not verbatim statutory text.
- The default local-fixture sample in `data/samples/` currently mirrors the Ulsan youth job-support case.
